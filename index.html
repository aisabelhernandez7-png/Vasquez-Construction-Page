🏡 Connect Your Google Sheet
Your community data lives in Google Sheets — anyone with access can add or edit. No code needed.
1
Make a copy of the template sheet
👉 Click here to copy the Princeton Communities template →

OR create your own Google Sheet with these columns in Row 1 (exact spelling):
builder series name status address price_low price_high agent phone email website features incentives tax_rate mud pid bac hoa hoa_includes tag fha va usda conv
2
Share the sheet publicly (view-only is fine)
In your Google Sheet: File → Share → Share with others
Change access to "Anyone with the link → Viewer"
This lets the app read data. Only people you invite as Editors can change it.
3
Paste your Sheet URL below
Copy the URL from your browser and paste it here. It should look like:
https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/SHEET_ID/edit...
